Pregnancy is a beautiful journey that lasts about 40 weeks, divided into three trimesters. In the first trimester, the baby's organs begin to form, and mothers may experience fatigue, nausea, and cravings. The second trimester often brings increased energy, a growing belly, and noticeable baby movements. In the third trimester, the baby gains weight and prepares for birth, while mothers may feel Braxton Hicks contractions and need more rest. A balanced diet, light exercise, and regular prenatal checkups are essential throughout pregnancy to support both mother and baby. Avoid harmful substances and focus on self-care for a healthy pregnancy.
